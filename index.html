Who are We?

The Ceyline Maritime Services (Myanmar) Co. Ltd was established in July 2018 in Yangon, Myanmar, obtained the SRPS license in Myanmar for Crew Recruitment and Placement. We are a Co-Member of Ceyline Holdings Colombo, Sri Lanka.

We are currently in the process of building our crew pool ranging across all ranks that is proficient to meet the demands of International Shipping Lines. Our crew pool is carefully selected by our experienced in-house team ensuring that all selected candidates receive the necessary training in accordance with IMO approved standards to stay abreast of all developments in the Marine Industry. It has been licensed by Department of Marine Administration (DMA) to provide its services.
